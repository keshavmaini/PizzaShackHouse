About Pizza Shack House

Serving fresh pizza, quality ingredients, and unforgettable taste in Amritsar since 2023.

Our Journey

The story behind Pizza Shack House is built on determination, hard work, and a passion for quality food. Before establishing Pizza Shack House in 2023, the founders gained valuable experience in the food industry through earlier ventures in Amritsar.

Along the journey, they faced several challenges, including operational difficulties, labour shortages, and the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic. Instead of giving up, the family stepped forward and personally managed the business during difficult times.

With dedication, perseverance, and support from family members, they rebuilt their dream and established Pizza Shack House at Batala Road, Amritsar. Today, the restaurant proudly serves customers with the same commitment to quality and customer satisfaction that inspired its beginning.

What Makes Us Different?

Signature Cheese Burst Pizza

Our famous Cheese Burst Pizza is crafted with rich cheese throughout the pizza, including the sides, creating a unique experience loved by customers.

Quality Without Compromise

Quality is our priority and purity is our goal. Every order is prepared with care and attention to maintain consistent taste and freshness.

Fresh Dough Preparation

We prepare our dough in-house instead of relying on ready-made pizza bases, ensuring freshness and quality in every bite.

Our Mission

To serve fresh, delicious, and high-quality food while creating a memorable dining experience for every customer.

Our Vision

To become one of the most trusted pizza destinations in Amritsar through quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
